Custom Made Joineries

We custom-make cabinets to your specifications and deliver them directly to your site.

The Ordering Process

01

Identify the Space

Determine the room or area where you need custom-made joinery.

02

Measure Your Space

Take the dimensions of your cabinet or joinery area.

03

Share Your Ideas

Provide any hand drawings, reference images, or specific design concepts you have in mind.

04

Initial Cost Estimate

Based on your measurements and ideas, we will provide a rough cost estimate. We can then confirm the on-site dimensions and provide more detailed CAD drawings.

05

Design Approval

We will create a detailed design for your joinery. If needed, we can provide 3D links or 3D drawings for your confirmation before proceeding to production.

06

Material and Color Selection

Once you approve the design, choose the material and color that best suits your vision.

07

Production

After finalizing the materials, we will send the design to the factory for production and arrange shipping to your location.

08

Installation

If installation is required, you can book a service with our professional team.
