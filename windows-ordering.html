Windows Ordering Process

Custom-Made Windows – Certified and Energy-Efficient Solutions

Our custom-made windows are manufactured in a licensed factory in Guangdong Province, China, ensuring high quality and compliance with Australian standards. Every window is certified to Sai Global AS 2047 and AS 1288, and our factory is a proud member of the Australian Glass and Window Association.

For New Builds

If your project is a new build, we will provide all required certificates for your building permit. Our windows are designed to meet 7-star energy rating requirements, and we will supply the necessary certification to ensure compliance.

For Renovations

If you are renovating and wish to upgrade from single glazing to double glazing, we offer:

  • On-site measurement for a precise fit
  • Supply and installation of energy-efficient windows
  • Consulting services if a building permit is required

Specifications & Customization

  • Frame and Finish: Aluminum Alloy Grade 6063-T5, fully compliant with Australian standards
  • Glazing: Certified to AS 4666, AS 2208, and AS 1288 (Low-E double glazing standard)
  • System: Window and Door System Certified to AS 2047
  • Upgrades: Thermally broken aluminum frames for superior insulation
  • Hardware: Choice of Chinese brand or Australian brand
