Port Melbourne

01 — Owner Builders & DIY Renovators

Meet Jenny & Gary

Jenny and Gary were eagerly anticipating their newest family member and needed a more spacious, functional home. Dreaming of an open kitchen and an extra bathroom, they turned to Melbourne Reno Pro.

Our architect meticulously crafted plans to reconfigure the layout, which involved removing a non-load-bearing wall and adding a new window to flood the kitchen with natural light. With a design certificate issued by our engineer, they successfully obtained their Building Permits without stress.

They opted for our custom-made cabinetry to maximize storage, conveniently shipped right to their doorstep. Gary tackled the cabinet installation himself, enlisting a licensed builder only for structural changes.

Result: A dream renovation tailored to their needs, on budget, with complete structural compliance.

02 — Volume Builders

Meet James & His Team

James is a local builder renowned for constructing volume homes, managing around 50 projects annually. He understands the importance of staying ahead of industry standards.

With the NCC 2022 and new 7-star energy rating requirements looming, James recognized the critical role of reliable window suppliers. He turned to Melbourne Reno Pro for bulk purchasing solutions.

Our licensed factory swiftly produced windows and glazing doors meeting AS2047 and AS1288 standards, fully certified by the Australian Glass and Window Association.

Result: Significant cost savings, boosted profitability, and guaranteed compliance across his entire portfolio of volume builds.
